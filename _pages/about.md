I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Circuits and Systems at Tianjin University, advised by Prof. Yu Liu.
My research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Architecture and Electronic Design Automation (EDA). Specifically, I focus on building autonomous and controllable technology systems for intelligent edge computing. My work addresses critical technical bottlenecks through: 
* Hardware-Software Co-design: Exploring agile design methods for reconfigurable spatial accelerators and RISC-V architectures.
* System Optimization: Working on architectural modeling, hardware-aware compilation, and intelligent scheduling algorithms.
I have published several first-author papers in top-tier journals and conferences, including IEEE TCAD, JSA, and ISEDA. My goal is to bridge the gap between complex algorithms and efficient hardware implementations.
